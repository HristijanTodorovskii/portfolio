Hello there,

I am Julija J. Marinov (ignoring the J.), and I am a marketing manager turned UX/UI designer.

Being always curious and passionate about design, I enrolled to the UX/UI Academy , which felt like a natural next step in my career. And as always, trusting my gut payed off, and I found myself glued to the computer working and creating.

With my previous experiences as a product manager, I became very detail oriented , responsible, committed to a project with a get-it-done attitude.
